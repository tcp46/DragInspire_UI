What is DragInspire?

DragInspire is a website aimed towards promoting the entrepreneurial setting at Drexel University. The purpose of this website is to enable students to connect and form groups to design and create the projects to gain experience and learn new things.

Students will be able to sign up via their Drexel email and create a user profile for others to see. In addition, users will have he ability to create ideas and projects directly on the website.

How does it work?

DragInspire contains an idea board that will hold all of the user-posted ideas. With this, students could browse the potential projects and reach out to those that they are interested in with a messaging system. In addition, there will be a matching system based on interests and skills to recommend for all students creating a project.

Why should you use it?

DragInspire is a great resource to get started on that project by finding peers with the skills that you need, as well as similar interests. It is also a great way to connect with people and learn new things!
